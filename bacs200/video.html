Why Climate
Change Matters

A video representing my view on climate change
and why we need to take action now.
(For BACS 200 Class)

  • Watch Video on Youtube
Script

Our world is vast, it’s beautiful and it’s the only home we have. If we fail on Earth we fail entirely and the fact of the matter is, as dramatic as it might sound. The fate of the world truly hangs in the balance. Climate Change has affected and will continue to affect the world around us if we don’t change the way we consume resources. The way we live our lives must change for the better if we hope to have the beauty of our planet stay intact for future generation and beyond. Right now, climate change has increased the progression and accelerated the rate at which mass extinctions of animals occur. For 8% of all animal extinctions will be because of climate change. The world is full of biodiversity but that can all change within the next few centuries. Climate Change affects everyone on this planet and though it is hard to pinpoint how exactly climate change will affect you personally, here are some examples. For starters everyone will experience harsher and much more extreme weather. The likeliness of food prices going up due to extreme drought has become much more relevant within the past century. Overall, further we head down this road without change will be the cause of the destruction for the natural world. The beauty around is fading away and its our duty as citizen of this planet to change that. We have the power to overcome any obstacles in our way, but only if we come together. Not as individuals but as a species. To find out more about climate change and how it changing the world around you, take a look at the resources within this blog. Now is the time for change and everyone can make a difference if they choose to do so.
