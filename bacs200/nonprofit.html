ABOUT US


Over the past 7 years of empirical research, we focused on leveraging persuasive technology to engage Millennials to adopt intrinsically sustainable lifestyle choices. We created Go Green for the purpose of incorporating innovative technologies to provide young people tracking, measurement, and monitoring capabilities to lead and maintain sustainable lifestyle habits.

    GO Green's Vision:
  • To inspire a youth culture concerned and motivated to act on behalf of global preservation
  • Create a student based population to educate, share, and understand sustainability
  • Bring about behavioral changes that will stimulate sustainability into communities in the real world.

Our research indicates that health and fitness presents a pivotal intersection to help our generation create sustainable lifestyles that start at an individual level, but can also be a fun, rewarding, and meaningful social activity. From here we plan to expand to other lifestyle areas including but not limited to consumption.

Capture The Value Proposition:

A foundation geared towards creating a sustainable future.


Describe the compelling vision in 50 words:

Go Greens primary vision is to educate students and future generations about the impacts of climate change and global preservation. They aim to bring behavioral change that will hopefully inspire the youth to seek out and engineer new paths towards clean and sustainable energy.


Three Strategies that will suport their vision:

  • Educate young people about climate change.
  • We were very successful and engaged more than 30k students to adopt sustainable lifestyles.
  • Create a student based population to educate, share, and understand sustainability.

    • How to get involved:

    For starters you can change the way you consume water and energy. Changing your habits to consume less and waste less resources is crucial in fighting climate change.

    One can also invest in energy-efficient appliances or better home efficient light bulbs and if applicable you can change the way you commute to work by car pooling or riding a bike if possible.

    You can also help out by donating to the foundation directly!
